Hello, I'm Rachel

I'm the owner of High Bridge Dog Retreat. Dogs have always been my biggest passion, and over 10 years ago I started getting involved in local dog rescues around the area.

To date I've fostered and homed 60+ dogs, adopting several of those fosters along the way. Since moving to a farm where I can host more pups, I've expanded into pet sitting.

I have a background in pet care and love every dog for their unique personality. You can have confidence that your dog will not only be safe but come home happy under my care.

The Property

Wide-open acreage to run by day, and a warm, comfortable home to unwind in by night.

Outdoor Space

A full 2.5 acres divided into 3 securely fenced yards, giving every guest room to run, sniff, and play safely — with space to separate pups when they'd prefer some quiet time of their own.

Indoor Space

A true in-home experience: couches, cozy dog beds, and kennels on request. Our luxury lower-level lounge is set apart from the office and living quarters, giving guests a calm, dedicated space to truly settle in and rest.
